Tunisia local data-center production

DarDev products and residency-sensitive workloads running in Tunisian data centers Hesabi, 20, and Control Plane components on local Kubernetes, alongside multi-cloud delivery elsewhere.

Context

Some DarDev products and client systems require data residency and direct ops control in Tunisia not everything belongs on a single public cloud region.

What we delivered

  • Local data-center Kubernetes for Hesabi, 20, and Control Plane components
  • Same GitOps, identity, and observability patterns as cloud environments
  • Hybrid routing international and elastic workloads on AWS, Azure, or GCP where appropriate
  • Production ops from the Tunis engineering bench across both models

Stack & patterns

Outcome

Products and clients that need Tunisia-hosted production have it without sacrificing the multi-cloud capability DarDev uses everywhere else.
